City or Nature?

One of the most evident difference between nature and city life is that the nature is filled with serenity and calmness whereas city life is filled with daily chaos and hustle-bustle. The city life is very busy, whereas being near to nature helps us to relieve from distress and anxiety. Nature is an important part of ecosystem, as it helps to maintain the balance in an environment. City life is polluted, whereas, nature is refreshing and filled with fresh air.
